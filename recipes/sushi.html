Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Wasabi paste
  • Smoked salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice
  2. Mix in the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate
  3. Assemble the sushi rolls
  4. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces
